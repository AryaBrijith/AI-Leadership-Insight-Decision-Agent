ADOBE - STRATEGIC PLANNING NOTES 2024-2026

VISION & STRATEGIC DIRECTION
Adobe's 3-year strategic plan focuses on three pillars:
1. Digital Transformation: Shift 60% of revenue to recurring SaaS/subscription model by 2026
2. Geographic Expansion: Enter 5 new markets in APAC and LATAM
3. Operational Excellence: Reduce cost-to-serve by 18% through automation and AI

GROWTH INITIATIVES

Initiative A: SaaS Platform Scaling
Current ARR: $500M. Target: $1.2B by end of 2026.
Investment required: $180M over 3 years.
Key milestones: European launch Q2 2024, Enterprise tier launch Q3 2024, APAC expansion Q1 2025.

Initiative B: AI & Automation Integration
Deploy AI-assisted workflows across Finance, HR, and Customer Success by end of 2024.
Expected efficiency gain: 22% reduction in manual processing time.
Partnership with CloudAI Inc. signed in September 2023 — $30M 3-year contract.

Initiative C: Supply Chain Resilience
Dual-source all Tier-1 components by Q3 2024.
Build strategic inventory buffer: 8-week safety stock (currently 3 weeks).
Evaluate nearshoring opportunities in Mexico and Eastern Europe.

DEPARTMENTAL STRATEGIC TARGETS (2024)
- Technology: Grow headcount by 15%, focus on AI/ML engineers
- Sales: Expand enterprise sales team by 20 reps in Q1 2024
- Logistics: Complete fleet modernization program; target 95% on-time delivery (currently 88%)
- Manufacturing: Achieve zero-defect rate improvement from 96.2% to 98.5%
- HR: Reduce engineering attrition from 22% to below 12% via new compensation and career ladder programs

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE
Primary competitors: TechGlobal Inc., NextGen Solutions, Apex Systems.
Adobe's differentiation: Deep enterprise integrations, regulatory compliance tooling, and 15-year customer relationships in core verticals.
Threat assessment: New SaaS entrants are price-competitive but lack enterprise-grade security and compliance features — this is Adobe's strongest moat.

FINANCIAL TARGETS
2024 Revenue Target: $4.9 billion (+17% growth)
2025 Revenue Target: $5.7 billion (+16% growth)
2026 Revenue Target: $6.8 billion (+19% growth)
Target EBITDA margin by 2026: 26% (from current 22%)
